We're not just another marketing agency. We dissect what makes brands grow — and engineer systems that scale.

The Marketing Anatomy was founded on a simple belief

Great social media isn't about going viral. It's about building a system that consistently turns strangers into followers, followers into leads, and leads into loyal customers.

We started in 2021 as a one-person consultancy, fueled by a frustration with the "post and pray" approach most businesses take on social media. Today, we're a focused team of strategists, content creators, and growth architects working with founders across industries.

Our process is hands-on and collaborative — combining clear strategy with creative instinct to create work that's thoughtful, lasting, and unmistakably yours.
